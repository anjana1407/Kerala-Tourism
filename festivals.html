Festivals in Kerala

Onam

Onam is an annual Indian harvest
and cultural festival related to
Hinduism celebrated mostly by the people of Kerala.

Vishu

Vishu is a cultural festival
celebrating the Malayali New Year
in Kerala.

Thrissur Pooram

The Thrissur Pooram is an annual
Hindu temple festival held in
Thrissur, Kerala, India.

Attukal Pongala

Attukal Pongala is a 10-day religious
festival celebrated at the Attukal
Bhagavathy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram in the
Indian state of Kerala.
